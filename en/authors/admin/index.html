Lucas Gelape

PhD Candidate

University of São Paulo

PhD Candidate, Political Science, University of São Paulo. Visiting fellow at Harvard University (09/2019 to 02/2020). MA in Political Science (2017) and Bachelor of Laws (2014), Federal University of Minas Gerais. Researcher at the Research Center for Comparative and International Studies (NECI-USP), and an affiliate to the Study Group on Local Politics (NEPOL-UFJF).

I contribute to Volt Data Lab’s projects as a data analyst. Worked as a data scientist on G1 between July and November, 2018, where I produced news about the Brazilian general elections.

In my website, you can access my academic research, find information on courses taught and on teaching assistant positions, developed softwares, and a blog about other works I have been involved with.
